## What Happened

### Viral Shorts Research
- Deep-dived into what makes YouTube Shorts and TikTok content actually go viral
- Key finding: real hook threshold is 2 seconds, not 3 — VVSA rate must hit 70%+
- YouTube now counts every replay as a view (as of March 31, 2025) — looping Shorts can hit 300%+ APV
- Captions increase completion by 12–15%; ~60% of Shorts watched without sound
- Full research → [[../02-Research/Shorts-Production/viral_shorts_analysis|Viral Shorts Analysis]]
- Distilled action items → [[../02-Research/Shorts-Production/viral_shorts_top10|Top 10 Viral Shorts Findings]]

### RelaxHistoryAI Channel Analysis
- Analyzed @RelaxHistoryAI — channel using AI to reconstruct photorealistic faces of historical figures
- Core thesis: "connection over information" — make viewers *feel* something about historical people
- Two sub-genres identified: Face Reconstruction (RelaxHistoryAI style) vs Historical Explainer (Ancient Civilizations style)
- Full analysis → [[../02-Research/Shorts-Production/relax_history_ai_analysis|RelaxHistoryAI Analysis]]
- Practical style guide → [[../02-Research/Shorts-Production/history_shorts_style_guide|History Shorts Style Guide]]

### Ranking Videos Research
- Researched the ranking/countdown video format for Shorts
- Built-in suspense (countdown structure), debate bait (#1 pick), universal appeal across languages
- Best performing: country comparisons, historical rankings, sports rankings
- Full notes → [[../02-Research/Shorts-Production/ranking_shorts_research|Ranking Shorts Research]]

### Viewmax.io Competitor Research
- Investigated Viewmax.io — AI short-form video tool using Sora 2 (OpenAI)
- Key features: text-to-video, AI scriptwriter, auto-captions, trending topic scanner
- Relevant as a competitor to YouTube AI Studio's feature set
- Full notes → [[../02-Research/Shorts-Production/viewmax_io_research|Viewmax.io Research]]
