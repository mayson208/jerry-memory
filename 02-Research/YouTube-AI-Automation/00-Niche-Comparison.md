## Roblox Gaming

**Rating: 8/10 — Good but proceed carefully**

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| CPM | $2–4 (drops to ~$0.30 if "Made for Kids" label applied) |
| RPM | $1.50–3 |
| Competition | VERY HIGH |
| Audience | Teens + young adults (13–24) |
| Automation | 9/10 — highly scriptable challenge arcs |
| Monthly cost | Low — minimal stock footage needed |

**The MFK warning:** YouTube's "Made for Kids" label kills ~95% of revenue. Content must be framed as teen/adult to avoid it. Every video should be positioned for 13–17+ audience, not children.

**Best format:** "I survived 24 hours in X" / "I tried Y" / challenge arc with setup, tension, resolution.

**Top 3 video ideas:**
1. Challenge/constraint format — "I Only Used Starter Items for 30 Days"
2. News/update style — "The Update That Changed Everything"
3. Comparison — "Free vs $1,000 Roblox Experience"

## Decision Framework

**Start with Indus Valley if:**
- You want first-mover advantage in an untapped niche
- You want to capitalize on the $1M script prize news hook (still has momentum)
- You're willing to use AI image generation to fill the footage gap

**Start with Mesopotamia if:**
- You want higher CPM from day one ($10–25 vs $4–15 blended)
- You want more stock footage availability
- You prefer a pure Western audience without diaspora traffic management

**Start with Roblox if:**
- You want faster subscriber growth (younger audience, more shareable)
- You're comfortable managing the MFK risk
- You want highest automation ease (no footage needed)
