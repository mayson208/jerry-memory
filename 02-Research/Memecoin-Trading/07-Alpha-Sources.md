## How to Vet an Alpha Source

1. Do they post losses as well as wins?
2. Do they share their wallet address openly?
3. Is their track record verifiable on-chain?
4. Do they have a paid group (conflict of interest)?
5. How long have they been calling? (1+ year > 1 month)
