# KOL Calls — Key Opinion Leaders

> [[00-INDEX|← Back to Index]]

---

## The Reality of KOL Calls (2025–2026)

- > 60% of KOL-backed projects see initial pump
- ~50% crash due to credibility issues within weeks
- ~90% fail to survive beyond 2 months
- **KOLs receive tokens at deep discounts (or free) before calling — you are their exit liquidity**

---

## How KOL Calls Work

1. KOL buys token (often at a discount or pre-call)
2. Posts CA on Twitter/Telegram
3. Followers rush to buy (FOMO)
4. Price spikes immediately
5. KOL sells into the pump
6. Price dumps, followers hold bags

---

## How to Use KOL Calls Without Getting Rugged

```
1. NEVER buy the first minute of a KOL call — price already pumped
2. Wait for the initial dump — panic sellers exit, price dips
3. Only buy the dip IF fundamentals support it (real community forming)
4. Size small — 0.1–0.5x your normal position on KOL calls
5. Exit fast — KOL calls are usually pump-and-dumps, don't hold overnight
6. Check if KOL has transparent track record (posts W AND L?)
```

---

## Spotting Conflicts of Interest

- KOL has paid "alpha group" (paid to shill)
- KOL doesn't disclose token holdings
- KOL suddenly posts a coin with no prior discussion
- KOL only posts winners (never admits losses)
- History of coins crashing after their calls

---

## Legitimate vs Predatory KOLs

| Legitimate | Predatory |
|-----------|----------|
| Posts analysis and reasoning | Just posts CA with hype |
| Admits mistakes publicly | Only posts wins |
| Verifiable on-chain track record | No wallet transparency |
| Consistent over time | Recent account or dormant between calls |
| No paid groups | Sells access to "alpha" |

## Rule of Thumb

> If a KOL is calling something loudly and it's already up 3x+ in the last hour — **you are the exit liquidity**. Move on.
