# Winning Patterns — What 10x–100x Coins Have in Common

> [[00-INDEX|← Back to Index]]

---

## The Core Formula

> **Strong narrative + active community + fair launch + right timing = moonshot candidate**

---

## 1. Narrative (Most Important Factor)

- Every successful memecoin tells a story people emotionally invest in
- Story must be: **simple, shareable, timely** (tied to current event/meme)
- Examples: TRUMP (political moment), GOAT (AI agent narrative), PEPE (meme culture)
- Coins that ride an active cultural moment **outperform generic launches 10:1**

---

## 2. Community Quality

- Active Telegram/Discord (real conversations, not price spam)
- Holders are advocates, not just speculators
- Dev engages with community (even pseudonymously)
- Organic Twitter content created by holders without being paid
- Community creates memes, videos, gifs spontaneously

---

## 3. Launch Characteristics of Winners

- Fair launch (no presale, no VC allocation)
- Bonding curve launch on Pump.fun (transparent)
- Dev wallet < 5% at launch
- Clean bundle/sniper stats (< 15% / < 25%)
- Liquidity burned or locked at graduation

---

## 4. Timing Indicators

- Launch coincides with hot narrative
- Early buyers are organic (not insider wallets)
- Volume growing steadily (not one whale)
- Holder count growing continuously
- Social mentions increasing independently across Twitter AND Telegram

---

## 5. Technical Patterns

- Consolidation after initial spike → second leg up = healthiest sign
- Coins that hold 50% of initial pump tend to run again
- Graduation from Pump.fun → 2x–5x spike = most reliable pattern
- Volume/mcap ratio stays healthy throughout the day (sustained interest)

---

## What DOESN'T Predict Success (Myths)

- ❌ Great meme image alone
- ❌ Large Telegram size alone (can be bought)
- ❌ High Twitter followers alone (can be bought)
- ❌ KOL shilling — ~50% crash within 2 months post-call

---

## Pattern Recognition Checklist

```
□ Does this coin fit a current hot narrative?
□ Is the name/ticker instantly shareable?
□ Are people creating organic content about it?
□ Is the community forming across multiple platforms independently?
□ Is the launch clean (bundle/sniper metrics)?
□ Is volume growing across many unique wallets?
□ Are smart money wallets accumulating?
```
