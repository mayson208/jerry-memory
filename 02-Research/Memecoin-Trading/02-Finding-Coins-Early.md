# Finding Coins Early

> [[00-INDEX|← Back to Index]]

---

## How Early is "Early"?

| Window | Who Can Play | Risk Level |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| 0–30 seconds | Bots only (200–400ms Turbine advantage) | Extreme |
| 1–10 min | Human snipers on Pulse/GMGN | Very High |
| 10–60 min | Still early if coin hasn't pumped | High |
| 1–4 hours | Momentum play if narrative strong | Medium-High |
| Post-graduation | Higher safety, lower ceiling | Medium |

> **Realistic human entry window: 1–10 minutes after launch**

---

## The 5-Step Early Entry Workflow

```
1. SPOT   — Axiom Pulse New Pairs or GMGN new token feed
2. CHECK  — RugCheck.xyz + Axiom Vision (mint/freeze/bundle/sniper)
3. VERIFY — Solscan holders + Twitter/Telegram organic check
4. CONFIRM— Any smart money wallets (GMGN) buying?
5. BUY    — Flash Buy, 10–15% slippage, MEV ON
```

3. **Dexscreener** — trending tokens, volume spikes
4. **Pump.fun** — live feed, manual scroll for brand new launches
5. **Twitter/X CT** — early organic mentions before mainstream
6. **Telegram alpha groups** — early calls (verify before acting)
7. **Wallet trackers** — GMGN, KOLSCAN watching smart money

## Full Manual Workflow (Step by Step)

1. Open Axiom Pulse, set filters (50+ holders, 3–30 min, $20K+ vol)
2. Spot a token with growing holders and volume
3. Open new tab → paste CA into RugCheck.xyz (30 seconds)
4. Check holder distribution on Solscan (holders tab)
5. Search Twitter for ticker/name — organic buzz or dead?
6. Check GMGN — any smart money wallets in?
7. If passes: buy small initial (0.05–0.1 SOL)
8. Watch momentum — holders and volume growing? Add to position
9. Set TP at 2x, 5x, 10x using Axiom TPSL
10. Exit fully if dev sells or Telegram goes silent

---

## What to Look For in First 5 Minutes

- Holder count growing (not flat)
- Volume spread across multiple wallets (not 1–2)
- No large single-wallet accumulation
- Twitter account exists and has organic posts
- Telegram active with real conversation (not bots)
- Bonding curve % accelerating

---

## Signs It's Too Late

- Already 10x+ from launch with no pullback
- Volume declining while price holds (distribution)
- Chart shows one giant green candle then lower highs
- Major KOL already called it and it's up 5x in 1 hour
- Telegram going quiet after initial hype
