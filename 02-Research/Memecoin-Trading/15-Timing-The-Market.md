> [[00-INDEX|← Back to Index]]

---

## Best Times of Day (UTC)

| Window | UTC | EST | Why |
|--------|-----|-----|-----|
| Early US morning | 12:00–16:00 | 7AM–11AM | Fresh retail liquidity, post-Asia review |
| **US afternoon/evening** | **20:00–00:00** | **3PM–7PM** | **Best — US + Asia overlap, highest total volume** |
| Dead zone | 03:00–09:00 | 10PM–4AM | Low volume, pumps can still happen |

---

## Best Days of Week

| Day | Quality |
|-----|---------|
| Tuesday–Thursday | Highest volume historically — best days |
| Monday | Often continuation of weekend moves |
| Friday afternoon | Volume drops as traders close for weekend |
| Weekends | Lower overall volume, but viral moments don't follow a schedule |

---

## How to Read Momentum (Buy Signals)

- Holder count growing (not flat)
- Volume consistent across multiple wallets (not one whale)
- Price making higher lows (each dip is bought up)
- Social mentions increasing across Twitter AND Telegram independently
- Bonding curve % rising steadily (approaching graduation)
- Buys >> Sells ratio on Axiom/Dexscreener
- Unique wallets growing (organic new buyers)

---

## When NOT to Enter

```
✗ Already 10x+ from launch with no pullback — likely last buyer
✗ Volume declining while price holds (distribution phase)
✗ Telegram gone quiet after initial hype
✗ Chart: one giant green candle then lower highs (classic P&D)
✗ Major KOL called it and it's already up 5x in last hour
✗ Same few wallets dominating volume (no organic spread)
```

## The Graduation Pump Timing

- Watch Final Stretch feed for coins at 90–99% bonding curve
- Buy when curve hits 95%+ and volume is accelerating
- Sell 50% into the graduation pump (2x–5x typically)
- Hold rest to see if post-graduation community forms
