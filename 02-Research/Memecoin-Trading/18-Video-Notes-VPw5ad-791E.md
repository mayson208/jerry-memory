## Key Concepts for Beginners (Inferred from Known Content)

### Getting Started
1. **Set up a crypto wallet** — Phantom wallet for Solana ecosystem
2. **Buy SOL** on a centralized exchange (Coinbase, Kraken, Binance)
3. **Bridge to wallet** — transfer SOL from CEX to Phantom
4. **Use a DEX** — trade on Raydium or via tools like Axiom.trade
5. **Start small** — never risk more than you can afford to lose

### Understanding Meme Coins
- Meme coins are high-risk, high-reward speculative assets
- Most lose value — the goal is to find the few that 10x–1000x
- Success depends on timing, community momentum, and narrative strength
- Solana meme coins (via Pump.fun) are the primary arena

### Risk Management
- Position sizing is critical — don't over-allocate to any single coin
- Take profits along the way — don't wait for the top
- Accept losses as part of the process — even experienced traders lose frequently
- "Protect your size" — as Choi emphasized after his $1M security incident

## Security Warnings (Critical — Alex Choi's Personal Experience)

Alex Choi lost ~$996,000 in a sophisticated phishing scam. His public warnings:

1. **"Stay paranoid"** — even experienced investors get targeted
2. **Do your research** on any project before engaging
3. **Store assets in cold wallets** — hardware wallets for large holdings
4. **Never join third-party video calls** from unverified sources (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet)
5. **Verify everything** — bad actors create convincing fake accounts and projects
6. Simply joining a video call can be the attack vector — no link click required
