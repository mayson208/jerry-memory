## How To Use This List

**The core strategy:**
1. Follow every Tier 1 account. Turn on X notifications for the highest-impact ones.
2. When a Tier 1 account posts about a coin you haven't heard of, open Axiom immediately and check the chart — the pump often starts within minutes of a post.
3. Cross-reference: if 2+ Tier 1 accounts mention the same coin within hours, that's a strong signal.
4. Use GMGN or KOLSCAN to verify the caller's wallet is actually in the coin before you ape — protects against pure shills.
5. Tier 3 accounts are for finding coins BEFORE Tier 1 picks them up. Follow them for early alpha, not confirmation.

**Red flags to watch for:**
- Caller posts about a coin but their wallet isn't in it (check GMGN/KOLSCAN)
- They call it, it pumps, then they go quiet — classic sell-into-pump
- No disclosure on paid promotions (ZachXBT has exposed 200+ for this)
- Account age < 6 months with no track record
- They only call coins that were already pumping

**Tools to pair with this list:**
- **KOLSCAN** (kolscan.io) — tracks KOL wallet activity in real time, acquired by Pump.fun
- **GMGN** (gmgn.ai) — smart money wallet tracking, SnipeX monitors Twitter accounts
- **TweetScout** (tweetscout.io) — scores crypto Twitter accounts by real influence
- **Axiom leaderboard** (axiom.trade) — top traders by PNL/win rate

## HOW THESE ACCOUNTS ACTUALLY MOVE THE MARKET

### Mechanism 1: Direct Pump (Tier 1 Accounts)
Ansem tweets about a sub-$1M market cap coin → his 600K followers see it → degens on CT ape in within seconds → price 5-20x in minutes. This is the most immediate effect.

### Mechanism 2: Narrative Validation (Tier 1-2)
Murad or Arthur Hayes mentions a coin → institutional and semi-institutional money follows → sustained buying over hours/days. Slower than Ansem but more durable pumps.

### Mechanism 3: KOL Cluster Effect
When 3+ accounts in Tier 1 or 2 all mention the same coin within 24-48 hours, it signals coordinated interest (whether organic or coordinated). This often precedes a major leg up. Watch for this pattern.

### Mechanism 4: On-Chain Leading Twitter (Best Alpha)
The smart money buys first, THEN the KOLs tweet. Tools like GMGN and KOLSCAN let you see wallet activity BEFORE the tweet. If you see an Ansem-linked wallet accumulate a new token → you can front-run his tweet.

### Mechanism 5: AI/Bot Amplification
Truth Terminal proved that an AI agent with a cult following (and a $50K Bitcoin donation from Marc Andreessen) can cause a 7,000% pump with zero human effort. Watch for AI agent accounts gaining traction.
